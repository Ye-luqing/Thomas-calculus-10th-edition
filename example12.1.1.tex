  \begin{example}
计算二重积分 $\displaystyle\iint_Rf(x,y)dA$,设
$$
f(x,y)=1-6x^2y,\mbox{且} R:0\leq x\leq 2,-1\leq y\leq 1.
$$
  \end{example}
  \begin{proof}[证明]
    利用 Fubini 定理,可得
$$
\iint_Rf(x,y)dA=\int_0^2\int_{-1}^11-6x^2ydydx=\int_0^22dx=4.
$$
  \end{proof}
